# TalTech Internship

## Focus areas

- what was built or delivered
- techniques and tools used
- decisions and tradeoffs
- what was learned
- how the work connects to TalTech studies

## Reporting standard

The report should make clear:

- what the task actually was
- what the student did personally
- what technologies and methods were used
- what results were produced
- what was learned

## Evidence expectations

Include concrete evidence where possible:

- features or deliverables
- screenshots or URLs
- repo or module references
- workflow steps

Make the report concrete, not vague or self-congratulatory.

## Expected output

Strong internship writing should clearly separate:

- context
- tasks performed
- technical decisions
- results
- lessons learned
