# TalTech Mathematics

## Focus areas

- clear derivation
- precise notation
- correctness of reasoning
- how the math connects to algorithms, probability, or data work

## Expectations

- define symbols and assumptions
- show enough intermediate reasoning
- connect the math back to the practical problem when relevant
- avoid vague "obvious" steps when they are not obvious

## Expected output

Good output should include:

- the problem statement in precise terms
- assumptions and notation
- the derivation or reasoning path
- the final result and what it implies

Do not hand-wave steps that a reviewer or lecturer would expect to see.
