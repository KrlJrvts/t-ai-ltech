# TalTech Thesis

## Focus areas

- a clear thesis objective
- justified method choice
- evidence for the solution
- readable academic structure
- a defense-ready summary of results and limitations

## Thesis standard

The thesis should make clear:

- what problem is being solved
- why the problem matters
- why the chosen method is appropriate
- what was implemented or analyzed
- what the results show
- where the work is limited

## Defense readiness

The student should be able to explain:

- major design decisions
- evaluation method
- limitations
- future work

The thesis must show independent reasoning, not just a built artifact.

## Expected output

A strong thesis-oriented output should contain:

- problem statement
- method or implementation approach
- evidence and evaluation
- limitations
- defense-ready summary
