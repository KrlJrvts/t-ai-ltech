# TalTech General Studies

## Focus areas

- ethics and professional responsibility
- sustainability and environmental impact
- communication quality
- business and entrepreneurship basics
- how software choices affect people and organizations

## Expectations

- connect technical decisions to professional consequences
- write clearly for non-specialists when needed
- treat sustainability and accessibility as engineering concerns, not decoration

## Good uses

Use this when a project or report needs:

- stronger ethical framing
- a sustainability section
- clearer professional communication
- entrepreneurship or business context

## Expected output

Good output should connect the abstract topic back to the concrete project:

- ethical or social implication
- stakeholder impact
- sustainability or accessibility consequence
- communication or business implication
